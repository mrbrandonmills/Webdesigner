# Dream Decoder AI - Product Hunt Launch Checklist

## Pre-Launch: 2 Weeks Before

### Product Readiness
- [ ] Product is fully functional and bug-free
- [ ] All features demoed in screenshots are working
- [ ] 3D visualization feature is stable and fast
- [ ] Mobile responsiveness is tested and working
- [ ] Error handling is graceful (no broken experiences)
- [ ] Analytics/tracking is set up to measure traffic from PH

### Product Hunt Submission
- [ ] Create Product Hunt maker account (if not already)
- [ ] Upload high-quality logo (240x240px, PNG)
- [ ] Write and finalize tagline (60 chars max)
- [ ] Write and finalize short description (260 chars max)
- [ ] Write and finalize full description
- [ ] Prepare first comment from maker
- [ ] Select relevant topics (AI, Psychology, Productivity)

### Visual Assets
- [ ] Create 6-8 gallery images (1270x760px recommended)
- [ ] Design thumbnail image
- [ ] Create animated GIF or video demo (optional but recommended)
- [ ] Ensure all screenshots show best features
- [ ] Add captions to all gallery images
- [ ] Test how images display on Product Hunt (light background)

### Hunter Coordination
- [ ] Confirm hunter (or decide to self-hunt)
- [ ] Send hunter all assets and information
- [ ] Brief hunter on key talking points
- [ ] Agree on exact launch time
- [ ] Exchange phone numbers for launch day communication

### Community Building
- [ ] Share teaser content on Twitter/X
- [ ] Post about upcoming launch on LinkedIn
- [ ] Reach out to supporters and ask them to bookmark launch day
- [ ] Create a launch day support list (names + contact info)
- [ ] Join relevant communities where you'll share (don't spam yet)

- [ ] Clear your calendar for launch day

### Final Asset Review
- [ ] Review all screenshots one more time
- [ ] Proofread all copy (tagline, descriptions, comments)

- [ ] Share to LinkedIn

- [ ] Message your support list with the link

### Morning Hours (6 AM - 12 PM PST)

- [ ] Respond to EVERY comment on Product Hunt
- [ ] Continue posting on Twitter/X (space them out)
- [ ] Engage with people sharing your launch
- [ ] Thank every person who comments or upvotes (publicly)
- [ ] Share behind-the-scenes content
- [ ] Monitor for any technical issues
- [ ] Post to relevant subreddits (follow rules carefully)
- [ ] Reach out to newsletters for potential coverage

### Afternoon Hours (12 PM - 6 PM PST)

- [ ] Continue responding to all comments
- [ ] Share progress updates ("We're #X on Product Hunt!")
- [ ] Post more Twitter/X content
- [ ] Engage with new supporters
- [ ] Share interesting comments/feedback publicly
- [ ] Take screenshots of milestones
- [ ] Send thank-you DMs to engaged supporters

### Evening Hours (6 PM - 12 AM PST)

- [ ] Maintain engagement on comments
- [ ] Final push on social media
- [ ] Thank your hunter and supporters
- [ ] Share final standings/results
- [ ] Begin drafting post-launch retrospective
- [ ] Screenshot final results for future reference

## Launch Day Engagement Guidelines

### Responding to Comments
- Respond within 30 minutes (faster is better)
- Be genuine and personal (not robotic)
- Thank people for trying the product
- Address criticism thoughtfully and constructively
- Ask follow-up questions to keep engagement going
- Upvote comments from genuine users

### Social Media Best Practices
- Don't just ask for upvotes (share value instead)
- Tell stories, share learnings, show personality
- Engage with replies, don't just broadcast
- Tag people you mention (but don't spam)
- Use relevant hashtags (#ProductHunt, #buildinpublic)

### Things to Avoid
- Don't ask for upvotes in exchange for something
- Don't create fake accounts to upvote
- Don't spam communities (follow their rules)
- Don't ignore criticism (address it gracefully)
- Don't disappearâ€”stay engaged all day

## Emergency Protocols

### If Site Goes Down
1. Don't panic
2. Post on Product Hunt comments immediately explaining
3. Share update on Twitter/X
4. Work on fix with highest priority
5. Update community when resolved

### If Negative Feedback Trends
1. Stay calm and professional
2. Acknowledge concerns genuinely
3. Explain your reasoning (without being defensive)
4. Promise to consider feedback
5. Follow up when you've addressed issues

### If You're Not Trending
1. Focus on genuine engagement, not vanity metrics
2. Reach out to your support list for help
3. Share more interesting content on social
4. Engage more deeply with commenters
5. Remember: users > upvotes
