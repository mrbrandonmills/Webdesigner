# 12-Month Content Calendar

---

## CALENDAR OVERVIEW

### Publishing Schedule

**Blog Posts:** 2-3 per month (24-36 annually)
**Email Newsletter:** 2-4 per month
**Instagram Posts:** 4-5 per week
**Instagram Stories:** Daily when relevant
**Facebook Posts:** 3-4 per week
**Product Launches:** 1-2 per month

### Content Pillars

1. **Product/Collection Content** (30%) - New releases, limited editions, product features
2. **Educational Content** (30%) - Photography tutorials, collecting guides, technical insights
3. **Behind-the-Scenes** (20%) - Process, stories, artist journey
4. **Community/Engagement** (20%) - Customer features, Q&A, interactive content

## CONTENT CREATION WORKFLOW

### Blog Posts
- Draft: 2 weeks before publish
- Review/edit: 1 week before publish
- Images prepared: 1 week before publish
- Schedule: 3 days before publish
- Promote: Publish day and following week

### Email Campaigns
- Plan: Monthly planning session
- Write: 1 week before send
- Design: 3 days before send
- Review: 1 day before send
- Send: Optimal time (Tuesday-Thursday, 10am EST)

### Social Media
- Plan: Monthly content calendar
- Create: Batch create weekly content
- Schedule: Schedule 1 week in advance
- Engage: Daily engagement and responses
