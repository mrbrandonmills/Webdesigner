# US - Works from the Collective Consciousness

Revolutionary AI-powered collective book writing platform landing page.

## Route
`/us-collaboration`

## Purpose
Convert visitors into contributors for Brandon Mills' collective book project where:
- Users share personal stories (voice, text, or files)
- AI processes and integrates all contributions
- Everyone becomes a published co-author
- Profit sharing for all contributors

## Features

### Hero Section
- Full-screen cinematic entrance
- Animated gradient orbs
- Parallax scroll effects
- Dual CTAs (Join + Learn More)
- Live stats (1,247+ contributors, 3 books, profit sharing)

### How It Works (4 Steps)
1. Share Your Story - Voice/text/files
2. AI Processes - Integration with collective
3. Become Published - Co-author credit
4. Earn Profit Share - Passive income

### Benefits (6 Value Props)
- Your voice in print
- Co-author credit
- Profit participation
- Zero writing effort
- Join a movement
- Hear your story (audiobook)

### Call to Action
- Email signup form
- Loading states
- Success confirmation
- Social proof (contributors, books, earnings)

### Footer Quote
- Inspirational message from Brandon Mills
