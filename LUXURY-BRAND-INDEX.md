## Key Strategies & Guidelines

### Pricing Philosophy

**The Golden Rules:**
1. Never compete on price - compete on value
2. Premium pricing signals quality
3. Entry products create discovery ($49-$149)
4. Core business is mid-range ($199-$499)
5. Luxury items create prestige ($1,999+)

**Target Margins:**
- Fine art prints: 60-75%
- Limited editions: 75-85%
- Digital products: 90-95%
- Services: 70-80%

---

### Limited Edition Strategy

**Critical Principles:**
1. Edition size is PERMANENT (never increase)
2. When sold out, it's sold out forever
3. Track every sale meticulously
4. Provide certificates of authenticity
5. Use scarcity in marketing

**Recommended Edition Sizes:**
- Experimental/Studio work: 15 prints
- Urban/Architectural: 25 prints
- Portraits: 50 prints
- Standard collections: Open edition

---

### Brand Voice Guidelines

**Use these words:**
- Museum-quality, archival, heirloom
- Limited edition, exclusive, rare
- Curated, timeless, iconic
- Exquisite, meticulously, precision
- Investment piece, collector's item

**Avoid these words:**
- Cheap, discount, sale
- Generic, standard, basic
- Mass-produced

**Tone:**
- Confident without arrogance
- Educational without condescension
- Exclusive without elitism
- Professional with personality
