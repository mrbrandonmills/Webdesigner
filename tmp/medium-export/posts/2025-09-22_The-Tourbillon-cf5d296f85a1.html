The Tourbillon

Did I take the rope off my neck just to put it around my wrist?

Inorganic elements

mimic a strangling fig vine,

an unnoticeable constant tightening.

Reassuring myself I endure —

non-natural ideas mixing with natural elements:

expectations with sweat, time with friction,

status with fear. These are my choices?

Yet I’m tied to something more than this physical.

This construct, man’s innovation holds on with a

cleverly constructed means to attach itself,

as if securing my neck in exchange for broadcasting my status.

By Brandon Mills on September 22, 2025.
