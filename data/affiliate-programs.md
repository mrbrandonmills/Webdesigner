## APPROVAL TIPS

### Before Applying:
1. **Publish 10+ quality blog posts** about photography, gear, techniques
2. **Add affiliate disclosure page** at /affiliate-disclosure
3. **Create "Recommended Gear" page** with placeholder content
4. **Ensure contact information** is visible
5. **Add About page** with professional bio
6. **Install Google Analytics** to track metrics
7. **Create social media profiles** if not already done

### Application Best Practices:
- Apply Monday-Wednesday (faster review)
- Use professional email address
- Be honest about traffic (don't inflate)
- Select relevant categories only
- Describe quality content plans
- Mention luxury/premium focus
- Highlight photography expertise

### If Rejected:
1. Wait 30 days
2. Add more content (20+ posts)
3. Increase social media presence
4. Get some organic traffic first
5. Reapply with improvements noted

## COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

### FTC Disclosure Requirements:
- Must disclose affiliate relationships
- Disclosure must be clear and conspicuous
- Place before affiliate links
- Use #affiliate or #ad on social media

### Sample Disclosure:
```
"This post contains affiliate links. If you click through and make a purchase,
I may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. I only recommend
products I personally use and believe in. Thank you for supporting my work!"
```
