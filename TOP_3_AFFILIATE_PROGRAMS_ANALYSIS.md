## RISK MITIGATION

**Challenge 1: Low Initial Conversions**
- Solution: Focus on building email list first, nurture then convert
- Implement 7-day email sequence before promoting products

**Challenge 2: High CPC Costs**
- Solution: Test interest targeting vs. lookalike audiences
- Create video content (typically 30% lower CPC)

**Challenge 3: Cookie Duration Limitations**
- Solution: Implement email retargeting sequences
- Create urgency with limited-time curated collections

- Solution: Apply to all three programs simultaneously
