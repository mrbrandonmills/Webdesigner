## III. Presentation Standards

### Photography Specifications

**Technical Requirements:**
- **Resolution:** Minimum 300 DPI at full size
- **Color Space:** Adobe RGB (wider gamut than sRGB)
- **File Format:** TIFF (lossless)
- **Bit Depth:** 16-bit (maximum tonal range)

**Printing Partner:**
- **Recommended:** [WhiteWall](https://www.whitewall.com) or [Hahnemühle](https://www.hahnemuehle.com)
- **Process:** Archival pigment printing (200+ year lifespan)
- **Paper:** Hahnemühle Photo Rag 308gsm or Museum Etching 350gsm

**Framing Partner:**
- **Recommended:** [Framebridge](https://www.framebridge.com) (luxury tier)
- **Options:** Museum float mount, UV glass, conservation matting

## IV. Scarcity & Exclusivity Mechanics

### Limited Edition Lifecycle

**Phase 1: Launch (Editions 1-3)**
- Premium pricing + VIP early access
- Personalized thank-you video from Brandon
- Behind-the-scenes story of the image

**Phase 3: Final Editions (Editions 9-10 for Tier 2, 4-5 for Tier 1)**
- Price increase by 20% (final edition premium)
- "Last chance" messaging
- Plate destroyed/file archived after final print

### Sold-Out Strategy

**When edition sells out:**
1. Display "SOLD OUT" badge on gallery
2. Waitlist for secondary market
3. Price displayed as "Last Sold: $5,000"
4. Archive page shows all sold editions with dates

**Secondary Market:**
- Brandon gets 10% royalty on resales
- Authenticity verification service
- Provenance tracking (who owned it when)

## XVI. FAQ for Customers

### About the Prints

**Q: What does "limited edition of 5" mean?**
A: Only 5 prints of this image will ever be produced. Once sold, the digital file is archived and no more prints are made — ever.

**Q: Are these hand-signed?**
A: Yes. Every print includes Brandon's signature, edition number (e.g., 3/5), and certificate of authenticity.

**Q: What's the lifespan of the print?**
A: Archival pigment printing on museum-grade paper has a rated lifespan of 200+ years under proper conditions.

**Q: Can I see these in person before buying?**
A: Currently online-only, but we offer a 7-day approval period. If you're not completely satisfied, full refund.

### About Shipping

**Q: How are $5,000 prints shipped?**
A: White-glove delivery service. Fully insured, custom crating, signature required. 2-3 weeks for production + shipping.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Yes. Shipping cost calculated at checkout. Import duties are buyer's responsibility.

### About Investment

**Q: Will these increase in value?**
A: Art is not a guaranteed investment. However, limited editions by emerging artists historically appreciate as the artist gains recognition.

**Q: Can I resell my print?**
A: Yes. Brandon requests 10% of resale price as a royalty (industry standard). We'll help authenticate for secondary buyers.
