## Common Rejection Reasons & How to Avoid

### Reason 1: "Site Not Established Enough"

**Fix:**
- Wait 60 days before reapplying
- Build traffic to 500+ monthly visitors
- Publish 10+ blog posts
- Get approved by 3-4 easier programs first

### Reason 2: "Content Not Relevant"

**Fix:**
- Add fashion/lifestyle content section
- Create "Style Guide" or "Curated Collections" page
- Show how you'll integrate affiliate products

### Reason 3: "Not Enough Traffic"

**Fix:**
- Run Google Ads for 30 days to boost traffic
- Focus on SEO (blog content)
- Share on social media consistently
- Aim for 100+ visitors/day before applying to selective programs

### Reason 4: "Trademark Bidding Concerns"

**Fix:**
- Explicitly state: "I will NOT bid on your brand name in paid search"
- Focus on content marketing and organic methods
- Never bid on "NET-A-PORTER," "SSENSE," etc. in Google Ads

## After Approval: Next Steps

### 1. Add API Credentials to .env.local

```env
# ShareASale
SHAREASALE_AFFILIATE_ID=your_affiliate_id
SHAREASALE_MERCHANT_TOKEN=your_token

# CJ Affiliate
CJ_PUBLISHER_ID=your_publisher_id
CJ_PERSONAL_ACCESS_TOKEN=your_token

# Rakuten
RAKUTEN_SITE_ID=your_site_id
RAKUTEN_TOKEN=your_token

# Awin
AWIN_PUBLISHER_ID=your_publisher_id
AWIN_API_TOKEN=your_token
```

### 2. Test Affiliate Links

- Generate test link for each program
- Click link and make test purchase (if possible)
- Verify tracking works in affiliate dashboard

### 3. Add Products to Your Site

- Use admin interface: `/admin/affiliates/products/create`
- Add 5-10 products per program initially
- Focus on best-sellers from each brand

### 4. Comply with FTC Guidelines

**Required Disclosure (add to all pages with affiliate links):**

```
"This page contains affiliate links. If you make a purchase through these links,
I may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. I only recommend products
I believe will add value to your life. Thank you for supporting Brandon Mills."
```

**Where to Add:**
- Top of product pages with affiliate links
- Footer of every page
- Blog posts featuring affiliate products

**General Rule:** Wait 60-90 days before reapplying

**Improve These Metrics First:**
- Monthly traffic: 2x current
- Content: Add 10+ blog posts
- Email list: Grow to 100+ subscribers
- Social proof: Get testimonials
- Other approvals: Get approved by 3+ easier programs
